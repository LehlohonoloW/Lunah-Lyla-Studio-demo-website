About Lunah Lyla Studio

Founded in 2021 by Nondumiso, Lunah Lyla Studio is a multidisciplinary practice specializing in major construction refurbishment projects & interior design.

Our focus is on delivering excellence in projects through innovative and sustainable solutions. We endeavor to establish environments that embodies a truly collaborative effort between the designer and the client.

Nondumiso skillfully integrates the innate visual appeal of her global travels into her design creations. Her current vision stems from a strong aspiration to rethink the concept of accessible luxury by creating emotive environments that have been carefully chosen with genuine authenticity and unwavering passion.
