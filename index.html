About the Studio

Founded in 2021 by Nondumiso, Lunah Lyla Studio is dedicated to redefining accessible luxury through authenticity and passion.

Our focus is on delivering excellence in projects through innovative and sustainable solutions. We endeavor to establish environments that embody a truly collaborative effort between the designer and the client.

Nondumiso skillfully integrates the innate visual appeal of her global travels into her design creations, creating emotive environments that have been carefully curated with genuine authenticity.
